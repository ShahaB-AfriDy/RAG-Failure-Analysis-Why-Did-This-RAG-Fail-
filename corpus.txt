Document 1: Academic Calendar 2023

The annual academic meeting for 2023 was held on June 15, 2023.
The fall semester began on September 1, 2023.
The university cafeteria started serving breakfast at 8:00 AM during the 2023 academic year.
Parking fees were $5 per day for students and $10 per day for visitors.

Document 2: Academic Calendar 2024 (Revised)

The annual academic meeting for 2024 is scheduled for June 20, 2024.
The fall semester will begin on September 3, 2024.
Starting in 2024, the cafeteria serves breakfast at 7:00 AM.
Parking fees have increased to $7 per day for students and $12 per day for visitors.

Document 3: Department Policy (Old Version – 2022)

Graduate students must submit their thesis proposal by March 1.
Late submissions are not accepted.
Students must complete at least 24 credits before graduation.

Document 4: Department Policy (Updated – 2024)

Graduate students must submit their thesis proposal by April 1.
Late submissions are accepted with advisor approval.
Students must complete at least 30 credits before graduation.

Document 5: Research Symposium Information

The Annual Research Symposium is held every June.
In 2023, it was held on June 18.
In 2024, it will be held on June 22.
The symposium is different from the academic meeting.

Document 6: Cafeteria Notice (Temporary Change)

Due to renovations, the cafeteria temporarily opened at 9:00 AM in January 2024.
Regular breakfast hours resumed in February 2024.

Document 7: Parking Regulation Notice

Faculty members park free of charge.
Students must display a valid permit.
Visitors must pay the daily parking fee at the entrance kiosk.

Document 8: Graduation Requirements Clarification

Credit requirements differ by enrollment year.
Students enrolled before 2023 follow the old credit rule.
Students enrolled in 2023 or later follow the updated credit requirement.
